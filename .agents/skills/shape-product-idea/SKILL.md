# Shape Product Idea

Read `../../working-backwards/WORKFLOW.md`. Work from the initiative when one exists.

1. Establish one primary customer, the situation they are in, the costly problem, and the meaningful change.
2. Define in-scope behavior, non-goals, constraints, and measurable or observable success signals.
3. Record assumptions, decisions, alternatives, and risks in `decision-log.md`; never convert assumptions into facts.
4. Update the phase to `drafting` when the core decision is clear. Hand off to `write-announcement`.
