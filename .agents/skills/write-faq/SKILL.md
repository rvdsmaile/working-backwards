# Write FAQ

Read `../../working-backwards/WORKFLOW.md` and use `../../working-backwards/templates/faq.md`.

1. Derive questions a skeptical prospective customer would actually ask after reading the announcement.
2. Answer plainly and consistently with the announcement; expose ambiguity rather than hiding it.
3. Include who it is for, what changes, how it works at the right level, limits, and next steps.
4. Save to `faq.md`; offer the optional `write-internal-faq` stage, then route to all three challenge skills.
