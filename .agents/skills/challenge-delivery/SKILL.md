# Challenge Delivery

Read `../../working-backwards/WORKFLOW.md`. Review without writing implementation code.

1. Identify delivery-critical assumptions, dependencies, capability gaps, operational risks, and missing acceptance evidence.
2. Keep the critique at decision level; do not prematurely create engineering tickets or estimates.
3. Add findings to `critique.md` using the shared table; give every finding a severity and status of `open`.
4. Do not resolve findings. Hand off to `synthesize-working-backwards`.
